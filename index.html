February

Sant Tulsidas

The Shock of Ratnavali

Tulsidas was once consumed by intense worldly love for his wife, Ratnavali. One night, he crossed a flooded river using a corpse as a raft just to see her. Her words changed him forever:

"If you loved the Divine with the same intensity you feel for this body of flesh and bone, you would be realized in an instant."
