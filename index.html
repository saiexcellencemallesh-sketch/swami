What is Bhakthi?

Bhakti is often mistaken for ritual, but it is actually the language of the soul. It is described in the scriptures as Parama Prema Rupaâ€”the very embodiment of supreme love. It begins when the seeker realizes that logic has limits, but the heart is infinite. "Devotion is the bridge where the seeker and the Sought become one."

To find the truth, we must strip away the lies. The song Jhoot Chor serves as a spiritual mirror, representing the Divine as a thief who steals our ego to reveal our light.
